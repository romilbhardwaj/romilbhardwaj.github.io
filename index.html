Romil Bhardwaj


I'm a Ph.D. candidate at UC Berkeley working with Prof. Ion Stoica. I am broadly interested in scheduling and resource management for machine learning workloads.

I'm building SkyPilot, an open-source framework to run machine learning jobs on any cloud easily and cost-effectively. If you'd like to contribute to SkyPilot, reach out to me!

Previously, I was a Research Fellow at Microsoft Research India, where I had a great time working on problems in modern systems and networks with the systems group. I obtained my B.Tech (with Honors) in Computer Science from IIIT-Delhi in 2015, where I worked on context-aware biometrics systems and open source energy monitoring systems.
